# ROADMAP — 16-Week Core Curriculum

Each week has a theme and 6 daily subtopics. `dayInWeek` 0–5 maps to subtopics 1–6;
`dayInWeek` 6 is a Review Day. See `COACH.md` for the date math and cycling rules.
